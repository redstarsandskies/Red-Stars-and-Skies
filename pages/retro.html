🌟 WHAT WE USED TO THINK VS NOW 🌟

✦ EXPLORING THE EVOLUTION OF COSMIC UNDERSTANDING ✦ How Our Views of the Universe Have Changed Over Time ✦

GEOCENTRISM vs HELIOCENTRISM

THEN (Medieval Era)

People believed Earth was at the center of the universe. Everything revolved around us. The sun, moon, and stars all danced around our little blue planet.

NOW (Modern Era)

We now know the Sun is the center of our solar system! Earth and other planets orbit around it. Our perspective shifted, and so did our understanding of our place in space.

FLAT EARTH vs SPHERICAL EARTH

THEN (Ancient Times)

Many ancient civilizations thought Earth was flat. Some believed there was an edge where the ocean fell off!

NOW (Space Age)

We've confirmed Earth is an oblate spheroid (slightly flattened sphere). We have satellite images and astronauts who've seen it themselves!

STARS as FIXED LIGHTS vs DISTANT SUNS

THEN (Pre-Telescope)

Stars were thought to be fixed on a celestial sphere. They were eternal, unchanging lights.

NOW (Astronomical Era)

We now understand stars are massive balls of gas undergoing nuclear fusion! They have lifespans and evolve over billions of years.
